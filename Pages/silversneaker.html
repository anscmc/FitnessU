Silver Sneaker

1. Visit FitnessU and bring your current health insurance card.
2. Use your new memberships card at the SilverSneakers kiosk.
3. Enjoy a complementary personal trainer session!

FitnessU is proud to participate in the Healthways SilverSneakers Fitness Program. SilverSneakers is a fun, energizing program that helps older adults take greater control of their health by encouraging physical activity and offering social events.

This award-winning program helps people who are eligible for Medicare or group retirees unlock the door to greater independence and a healthier life. Many health plans around the country offer the SilverSneakers program.
